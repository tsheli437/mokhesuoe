Co-Founder Shadrack

About Shadrack

Shadrack joined IWB Recycling as the second CEO in 2024, bringing strategic leadership to expand the company's electronic waste recycling operations across Southern Africa. His partnership with Co-Founder Kenneth accelerated IWB's growth, securing international partnerships and investors.

Under his guidance, IWB became a pioneer in recycling computer components such as RAM, hard drives, and motherboard parts, contributing to sustainable practices in the region. His focus on operational efficiency and stakeholder engagement has strengthened IWB's market presence.

Shadrack's vision aligns with IWB's mission to reduce environmental impact through responsible e-waste management, positioning the company as a leader in African sustainability initiatives.
