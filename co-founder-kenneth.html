Co-Founder Kenneth

About Kenneth

Kenneth is the visionary Co-Founder of IWB Recycling, a leading company in electronic waste recycling in Southern Africa. Founded in 2024 with a capital of M100,000, Kenneth spearheaded initiatives to recycle computer parts such as RAM, hard drives, and motherboard components.

In his journey to secure funding, Kenneth partnered with Shadrack, who joined as the second CEO of the company. Together, they expanded IWB's reach beyond Lesotho, attracting investors and clients from across the region.

Kenneth's leadership has positioned IWB as a pioneer in electronic recycling and sustainability, making significant contributions to environmental conservation in Africa.
